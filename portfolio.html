Woodland Haunt

Woodland Haunt

Woodland Haunt, created on Ableton Live 12 Suite in the Summer of 2024, utilizes tracks created using audio effects – such as compressor and EQ eight – MIDI effects, and instruments. With overlapping pre-made ambience tracks, 3D enhancement, and sound effects like glass shattering, this song is an immersive experience reminiscent of a magical journey through the woods. Woodland Haunt is fitting as background music for any such plot and creates a suspenseful, yet light musical environment for listeners. The song begins with a single track and grows in complexity as it reaches a peak bridge. At the bridge, all tracks fade away before reappearing for the outro again, closing out one-by-one until a singular track is left. The intermittent sound effects create a sense of an active environment with random objects intervening as one journeys through the song. The initiating and closing sound of glass-shattering creates a sense of foreboding as though this song is played in the middle of a tragic event, perhaps reminding one of a memory in the midst of tragedy. This is enunciated by the twinkling chimes in the bridge which further enhance a magical, whimsical feeling in the middle of the song that one could associate with nostalgic memories.

How to Fill a Bucket

How to Fill a Bucket

Inspired by the children’s book "Have You Filled A Bucket Today?", this animation represents the act of spreading joy by passing positivity from your bucket to someone else’s. As a child, this children’s book was an impactful metaphor to being a light in people’s lives, even random passersby. It also served as a reminder that we have the power to dip into other people’s buckets too, and so we should be careful with our words and actions so we don’t leave others’ buckets empty. As in the season of giving, I created a winter setting for the December holiday season with matching music to recreate the joyful holiday spirit. The animation works by moving a smiling icon from one happy bucket to another sad bucket and when it is dropped in, the animation begins to play the song and the sad bucket begins to smile. Additionally, the initial bucket which is full of smiles also begins to glow when another bucket is filled. Other animations include the color changing feature of the smiling icons which makes the smiling bucket appear rainbow like with its array of colors.
